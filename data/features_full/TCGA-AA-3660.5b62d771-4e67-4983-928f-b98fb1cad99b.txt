Diagnosis/ diagnoses:

Colon and rectosigmoid resection material show an ulcerated colon carcinoma
characterized histologically as a moderately differentiated colorectal adenocarcinoma,
extending to within 3.5 cm of a resection margin, probably the aboral resection margin,
encircling the colon wall at a length of 6 cm. Invasive tumor spread tumor within all
intestinal wall layers to the bordering mesocolic fatty tissue. The oral and aboral resection
margin is tumor-free. Thirty-three mesocolic lymph nodes are tumor-free with
uncharacteristic reactive lesions.

Tumor stage: pT3 pNO (0/33) L0 V0; G2.
