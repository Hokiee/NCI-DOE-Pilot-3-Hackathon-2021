Pathology Report for Subiect-

Microscopic Description: Sections demonstrate a moderately hypercellular
glial neoplasm that is composed of protoplasmic astroctyes, gemistocytes,
microgemistocytes and oligodendroglioma. Focal hypercellular clustering is
seen. Atypia is generally mild but is focally moderate. Only a few scattered
mitotic figures are seen. Microvascular proliferation is present but necrosis
is not seen. There is focally prominent subpial accumulation and invasion
into the subarachnoid space is also noted.

Diagnosis:
Anaplastic Oligoastrocytoma, Astrocytoma predominant (WHO grade III).
