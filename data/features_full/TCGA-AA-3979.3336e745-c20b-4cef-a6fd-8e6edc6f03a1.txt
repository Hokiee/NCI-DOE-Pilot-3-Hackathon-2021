Diagnosis:

Resected section of colon (sigma) with a colon carcinoma characterized histologically as a
moderately differentiated colorectal adenocarcinoma, extending up to 5 cm to one resection
margin and measuring 4.5 cm in diameter at the widest point. Invasive spread of the tumor
within all intestinal wall layers up to the bordering mesocolic fatty tissue and the level of
the subserosa.

In addition, the colon wall has a few pseudodiverticula and slightly scarring
peridiverticulitis.

The stage of the tumor is therefore: pT3 pNO (0/22) L0 V0; G2 R0.
