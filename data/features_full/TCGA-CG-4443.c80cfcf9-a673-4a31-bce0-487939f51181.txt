Diagnosis/diagnoses:

Total gastrectomy preparation with inclusion of a moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma
of the stomach (intestinal type) situated in the corpus and showing polypous exophytic growth
with infiltration of the tunica submucosa. Tumor-free regional lymph nodes. Tumor-free oral
and aboral resection margin. Tumor-free omentum. In the antrum also circumscribed

relatively high-grade mucosal edema with pseudopolypous foveolar hyperplasia and in the
junction between the antrum and the corpus a small lipid islet.

Tumor stage: pT1 pNO (0/30) pMX; I
