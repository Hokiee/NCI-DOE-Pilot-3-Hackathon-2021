MICROSCOPIC DESCRIPTION
Microscopic sections of the right temporal brain tumor
reveals a malignant astrocytic neoplasm characterized by
proliferation of multinucleated malignant astrocytic cells with
hyperchromasia, nucleomegaly and frequent mitotic activity. The
tumor exhibits neovascularity and large geographic areas of
necrosis. Based on the histologic features, it is characterized as
a Glioblastoma multiforme (Astrocytoma WHO grade IV).
