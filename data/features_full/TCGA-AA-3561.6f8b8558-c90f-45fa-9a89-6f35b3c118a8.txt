Diagnosis:

Right hemicolectomy preparation with ulcerated colon carcinoma of the histological type
of a moderately differentiated colorectal adenocarcinoma, located in the area of the passage
between the cecum and ascending colon, encircling the intestinal wall and measuring

4.5 cm in length. Invasive spreading of the tumor within all intestinal wall layers to the
bordering mesocolic adipose tissue.

Appendix with fibrous obliteration of the lumen.

Tumor-free oral and aboral resection margin as well as greater omentum. Fifty lymph
nodes in the region tumor-free with uncharacteristically reactive changes.

Tumor stage therefore pT3 pN0 (0/50) L00 V0: G2 R0.
