Pathology Report for Subiect:-
Diagnosis: Anaplastic Astrocytoma (grade III)

Microscopic Description: Sections demonstrate a markedly hypercellular glial neoplasm
that is composed of cells resembling moderately atypical fibrillary astrocytes. Scattered
mitotic figures are seen with 11 mitoses seen in 10 high power fields. There is no
microvascular proliferation or necrosis. Definite oligodendroglioma differentiation is not

seen.
