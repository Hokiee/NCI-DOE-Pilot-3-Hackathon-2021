Diagnosis/ Diagnoses

1. Mechanically altered liver tissue with sections of a regressively changed cyst with
partially dystrophic wall calcification.

2. Subcapsular liver tissue with circumscribed, clearly regeneratory, nodular
hyperplasia and moderate steatosis.

3. Resected ileocolic sample with a moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma of the
colorectal type, measuring max 4 cm, with infiltration of the tunica muscularis
propria up to the border of the perimuscular fatty tissue. Tumor-free local lymph
nodes. Tumor-free small and large intestine resection margins. Tumor-free
mesenteric resection margin. Complete, scarred obliteration of the appendix with a
condition following previous appendicitis.

The tumor stage is: pT2, pNO (0/23), pMX; G2, L0, V0, R
