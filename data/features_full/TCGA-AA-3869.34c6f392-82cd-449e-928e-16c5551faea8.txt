Diagnosis

Resected ileocolic sample with an ulcerated, moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma of
the colorectal type, measuring max. 7 cm in diameter, covering the cecum extensively, with
broad infiltration of the pericecal fatty tissue, circumscribed penetration of the covering
serosa and 14 local lymph node metastases. Tumor-free small and large intestine resection
margins. Tumor-free mesenteric resection margin. Tumor-free appendix.

The tumor stage is pT4 pN2 (14/28) pM1 (clinical liver metastases); G2 L1, V0, R2
