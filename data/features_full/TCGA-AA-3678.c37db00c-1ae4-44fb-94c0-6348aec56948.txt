Diagnosis:
1.: Colon resection material (sigmoid) with tumor-free, oral and aboral resection margins
includes an ulcerated, moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma with infiltration of the lamina
muscularis propria and solitary regional lymph node metastases (G2, pT2 le 1/26 L1, V0, R0).
2.: Inﬂammation-free small intestine or Meckel’s diverticulum without evidence of tissue
heterotopy.
