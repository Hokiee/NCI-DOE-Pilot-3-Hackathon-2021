Diagnosis

Resected colon material with an ulcerated, slightly differentiated adenocarcinoma of the
colorectal type, located 3 cm from a resection margin, with infiltration of the pericolic fatty
tissue, circumscribed penetration of the covering serosa as well as 23 local lymph node
metastases. Partly angioinvasive tumor branches, extending up to the mesenteric
resection margin. Tumor-free colon resection margins.

Tumor classification: pT4, pN2 (23/29), pMX; G3, L1, V1, R1.
