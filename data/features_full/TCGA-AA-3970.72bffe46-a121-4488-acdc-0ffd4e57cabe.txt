Diagnosis:

Left hemicolectomy preparation with a moderately differentiated, invasive adenocarcinoma
with infiltration of the pericolic fatty tissue. There is a further tubulovillous adenoma with
focal, severe dysplasia (synonym: severe intraepithelial neoplasia).

The tumor classification of the adenocarcinoma is: G2, pT3 N0 (0/27) L0 V0 R0.
