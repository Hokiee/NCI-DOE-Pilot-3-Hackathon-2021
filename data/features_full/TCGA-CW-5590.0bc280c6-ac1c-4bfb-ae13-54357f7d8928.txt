necrosis is present. Sarcomatoid differentiation is absent. The
surgical margins are negative. There are (11) additional separate
renal cell carcinomas, clear cell type, grade 1—2 (of 4) forming
tumor nodules ranging in size from 0.4 to 1.5 cm in greatest
dimension, scattered in the upper and mid portion of the kidney. No
renal hilar lymph nodes are identified.. The adrenal is
unremarkable.
