Main diagnosis/diagnoses:

Locally advanced, peripheral bronchopulmonary, histologically mixed adenocarcinoma of the
right upper lobe of the lung with a predominant large-cell, polymorphocellular and partly
clear-cell solid component, with bronchioloalveolar growth pattern in the tumor periphery and
very small acinar part, mainly located in S1 and satellite focus in S3.

TNM classification pT4 pN3 L1 V1 R0, stage IIIB

C 34.1 M 8255/3

Comment/supplementary remark:

The main tumor shows a large cicatricial area and has invaded small blood vessels located in
the tumor and also lymph nodes. There are lymph node metastases in samples 1.) to 4.), 6.) to
8.) and 10.). The visceral pleura and resection margins of the bronchus and vessels of the
main preparation and also the lymph nodes of samples 5.) to 9.) are tumor-free.
